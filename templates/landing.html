WHY SAFESPACE UG

Help that respects your boundaries

Designed specifically for University of Ghana students - because getting support should neve feel risky.

You stay anonymous

Counsellors only see a randomly generated identifier — never your name, student ID, or any personal details. Seek help without fear of being recognized.

Peer support without exposure

Connect with fellow students who understand what you're going through. Share, listen, and grow — all without revealing who you are.

No stigma, no judgment

Mental health struggles are human. SafeSpace UG removes the social barrier so you can reach out freely, on your own terms, at any time.

Crisis support, always on

If you're in distress, immediate crisis support is available around the clock — connecting you with trained professionals who care.
